"What Camera do you currently shoot with?

Currently, I use a Nikon D500 equipped with a Sigma 18-35mm lens.

"When did you first pick up a camera?"

I first Picked up a camera when I was in elementary school. My dad and older brother took me to a car show and they let me play around with their cameras. I would do this weekly until I was able to afford my own beginner camera in middle school. I have been shooting for about 8 years.

"What is special about photography?"

To me, I find that photography provides me with a creative outlet and somwhere where I can capture anything I really want. I find it facinating that such an object can capture the presense of anything with a click of a small button. I think what makes photography and cameras especially so special to me is that given a camera to anyone, the first reaction is to turn it on and try to take some pictures of their surroundings and to figure out what all the little scroll wheels, buttons, and each screen do.
